# Go-To-Market Strategy

---

## Target Customer

### Primary — Independent Padel Clubs

- Club owners or managers running 2–20 courts in Europe (Spain, France, Italy, UK) or LATAM (Argentina, Mexico, Brazil)
- Currently managing bookings via phone calls, manual WhatsApp coordination, or a booking app their players resist using
- Typically 1–3 staff; time-poor, not technical

### Secondary — Small Club Chains

- Groups of 2–5 clubs under shared ownership, expanding and looking for standardised tooling
- Have a head office manager who handles IT/tools decisions

### Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

> A padel club owner in Spain who has a WhatsApp group of 200 players, spends 2+ hours a day responding to "Is Court 3 free tomorrow at 7pm?" messages, and uses CourtReserve or Playtomic for reservations but players rarely use the app.

---

## Value Proposition

| For Players | For Club Operators |
|---|---|
| Book in 30 seconds via WhatsApp — no app download | Stop answering booking messages manually |
| Available 24/7, even when the club is closed | All bookings logged and visible in one dashboard |
| Responds in natural language — no forms to fill | Plugs into existing booking system (no migration) |
| Instantly gets confirmation with booking reference | AI handles cancellations and availability queries too |

**Core message:** *"Your players already use WhatsApp. Now they can book courts there too."*

---

## Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | What they do | Why we win |
|---|---|---|
| Playtomic / CourtApp | Full booking apps with player-facing mobile app | Requires player download and account creation. We have zero player friction. |
| Generic WhatsApp chatbot builders | Visual flow builders for customer service bots | Not padel-specific, no booking system integration, no AI reasoning. |
| Manual WhatsApp groups | Club owner responds to messages in a group chat | Our direct replacement. We automate exactly what they're doing today. |
| Email/phone booking | Old-school reservation by call or email | Async, slow. We give players instant 24/7 response. |

**Key differentiator:** We are the only solution that combines (1) zero player friction (WhatsApp, no app), (2) AI natural language understanding, and (3) native integration with existing booking platforms.

## Sales Motion

### Direct Outreach

1. Identify target clubs via Google Maps (search "padel club [city]"), padel directories (Playtomic club listings), and LinkedIn
2. Find the owner/manager on LinkedIn or via the club's Instagram/website
3. Opening message: "I noticed your club uses [CourtReserve/Playtomic]. We built an AI agent that lets players book courts via WhatsApp in 30 seconds — zero new apps for them. Happy to show you a 5-minute demo?"
4. Demo via the `/test` simulator page — show a live booking conversation in real time

### Free Trial

- 30-day trial with full setup included (we help with 360dialog setup, booking config, webhook)
- Weekly check-in during trial to surface issues and gather feedback
- Conversion ask at day 21: "Are you seeing value? Here's the link to start your Starter subscription."

### Referral Programme

- Active club recommends to a partner club → both get one free month
- Clubs within the same padel community or franchise are natural referral vectors
- Simple referral code tracked via UTM on the signup page

### Community & Events

- World Padel Tour events (sponsor or exhibit at trade expos)
- Padel trade press (Padel Alto, Padel World Press) — product features and case study placements
- Local padel federations (Real Federación Española de Pádel, FIP) — partnership and endorsement
- Padel-focused Facebook/WhatsApp groups for club owners — organic community presence

## Launch Strategy

### Phase 0 — Pilot (Months 1–2)

- Recruit 3–5 pilot clubs (friends, warm intros, local clubs) — free in exchange for feedback and a case study
- Clubs must be using CourtReserve or Playtomic (supported adapters)
- Hands-on setup and support; gather every issue and friction point
- Success metrics to track: bookings processed, time saved per week (self-reported), player messages handled without human intervention

### Phase 1 — Soft Launch (Month 3)

- Publish case study from pilot club(s): "Club X reduced WhatsApp booking messages by 80%"
- Launch landing page with case study, pricing, and 30-day trial CTA
- Begin direct outreach to 50 target clubs in Spain (highest padel density in Europe)
- Onboard first 10 paying clubs; personally support each

### Phase 2 — Growth (Months 4–6)

- Activate referral programme
- Submit to padel-related directories and SaaS listing sites
- Run LinkedIn and Instagram ads targeting padel club owners/managers in Spain, France, Italy
- Aim: 30 paying clubs, €3k MRR

### Phase 3 — Scale (Month 6+)

- Self-serve onboarding (no manual setup required)
- Expand to LATAM (Argentina, Mexico) — translate landing page, support Spanish-language clubs natively
- Partner with 360dialog for co-marketing (they benefit from new WABA activations)
- Aim: 100 paying clubs, €10k MRR
